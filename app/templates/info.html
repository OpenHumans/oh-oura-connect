OH Oura Connect

The Oura ring is a wearable device that tracks sleep and activity data. This project connects your Oura account to Open Humans to export your Oura data to Open Humans, including: age, weight, gender, activity, sleep, temperature, and heart rate.

This data transfer app is operated by Open Humans Foundation and is governed by the Open Humans Terms of Use.

To learn more about the app's management and more, please see the FAQ.
